# Fantasy-Cricket-league
Fantasy Cricket It is an online game where you create a virtual team of real cricket players and score points depending on how your chosen players perform in real life matches. To win a tournament, you must try and get the maximum points and the No. 1 rank amongst other participants. Fantasy cricket project gives virtual team visualization of database for real cricket players and their score points in various matches throughout their career.
